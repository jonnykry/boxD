Welcome to boxD!

boxD is a multiplayer version of the game Dots and Boxes.

To play, select a nickname and begin placing lines.

Take note of the timer in the bottom-left of your screen.

The inner ring is your cool-off timer. This timer is triggered when you place a line, or when you attempt to place a line in an incorrect position. When the inner ring is red, you cannot place a line.

PLACE YOUR LINES WISELY, as other players may be prepared to steal your boxes.

The outter ring is the time you have to place a line before receiving a cool-off timer.

With that, you're ready to play! Enter a nickname below and begin closing boxes.



Good luck!
